We're not in the buzzkill business.

Throughout a typical month, there are days where we feel more energized than others. It's on these days that we are most creative, outgoing, and open to new experiences. For these parts of your cycle, we have selected special strains that gently enhance this bright sunny mood. tokn works with your body to keep you on cloud 9, instead of mellowing out your most exuberant days.

Bring your best self, even on your worst days

We know the drill - low energy, mood swings, anxiety. The lows of each month can leave us exhausted at best and downright ruined at worst. We can't wave a magic wand and make it all disappear, but...tokn can help take the edge off. On these cloudy days, tokn helps level out the emotional rollercoaster. Physical symptoms are dulled, and we sprinkle in a little enery boost for good measure.

Customized to you

Your hormones change every day, so tokn uses your cycle information to create the ideal sequence and dosage of CBD.

Everybody is different, so why aren't there products tailored to us individually that help us chill out or feel awesome? We think that's messed up, so we created a custom solution that works with you to bring out your best.

Small but mighty

If there's one thing we know a busy girl needs it's time. daily tokn is the perfect addition to your ritual - short and sweet, it's a meaningful moment in your day.
