So what is CBD?

CBD (cannabidiol) belongs to a class of ingredients called cannabinoids, and is one of over 113 active compounds found in the cannabis plant. Good news - it doesn't come with all the red tape of other Cannabis compounds.

What's the hype about CBD?

There are many studies exploring the health benefits of CBD including:

  • Stress relief
  • Reduced anxiety
  • Reduced inflammation
  • Decreased muscle and joint pain
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Natural focus
