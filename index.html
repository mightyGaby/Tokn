Naturally Effective

Each month of Tokn is inspired by seasonal and biological factors that can throw us off balance. The two primary cannabis strains are Cannabis Indica and Cannabis Sativa. We use specially selected combinations of each strain to achieve the perfect microdose for gentle effects.
Indica

Higher in CBN, Indica is the yin to Setiva's yang. It's relaxing qualities help alleviate:

  • Insomnia
  • Pain
  • Anxiety
  • Muscle Spasms
  • Headaches
Sativa

Indica's sister, Sativa, contains higher amounts of the THC cannabinoid. We love Sativa for its lighter qualities that help with:

  • Fatigue
  • Distraction
  • Mood Instability
  • Depression
